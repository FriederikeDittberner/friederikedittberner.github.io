Research interests

I am interested in geometric flows. In particular, mean curvature flow with a global forcing term. That is, for example, the enclosed volume preserving and surface area preserving mean curvature flow for hypersurfaces, and the enclosed area preserving curve shortening flow and the length preserving curve flow for curves.
